Liquid 3 is a biotechnological urban solution that replaces traditional trees with tanks filled with microalgae. These tanks absorb CO₂ and release oxygen, just like trees, but in a more space-efficient manner. Liquid 3 is especially useful in urban areas with limited space for planting.

Key features:
- CO₂ Absorption
- Oxygen Production
- Urban-Friendly Design
- Requires electricity and maintenance

Use Cases:
- Cities with air pollution
- Smart city projects
- Educational awareness

Developed in Serbia, it is considered a green innovation tackling urban climate challenges.
